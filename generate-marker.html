Generate Star Marker Pattern

To use your star image as an AR marker, you need to generate a pattern file (.patt). Follow these steps:

  1. Click the link below to open the AR.js Marker Generator
  2. Upload your star image (the black star on white background)
  3. Wait for the pattern to be generated
  4. Download the generated .patt file
  5. Save it as markers/star-marker.patt in your AR folder
  6. Refresh your AR app and test it!

Tips for Best Results:

  • Your star image (black on white) is perfect for marker generation
  • Print the marker at least 8x8 cm (3x3 inches) for best detection
  • Ensure good lighting when using the marker
  • Keep the marker flat and steady
